Lumina Aesthetics — Team and Providers

Team Philosophy

Our team is built around medical oversight, skill, and a shared philosophy: natural-looking results, safety first, and honest communication. Every client meets with a provider who can assess goals, anatomy, and candidacy. We do not use a sales-driven model; recommendations are based on what is appropriate for you.

How Consultations Work

Your first visit typically includes a conversation with a consultation coordinator and then time with a clinical provider. The provider will review your goals, medical history, and skin or facial anatomy. They will explain options, downtime, and pricing. Same-day treatment may be possible when it is safe and when you have had time to consider the plan—but it is never required.

Medical Director

Medical Director

Lead Injector

Trust and Credibility

Our providers are licensed, trained, and experienced in their respective areas. We do not use inflated titles or fake awards. Bios are accurate and verifiable. If you have questions about who will treat you or what their background is, we are happy to share that during your consultation.
