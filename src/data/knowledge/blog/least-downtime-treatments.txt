Which Treatments Have the Least Downtime?

Lumina Aesthetics — Raleigh, North Carolina

Introduction

If you have a busy schedule or an event coming up, you may want to know which treatments let you get back to normal quickly. Here’s a practical overview of low-downtime options at Lumina.

Neuromodulators (Botox-Type)

Typically minimal downtime. You may have small bumps for a short time; we ask you to avoid rubbing the area and strenuous exercise for about 24 hours. Most people return to work and social activities the same day.

Hydrafacial and Light Facials

These are designed for minimal downtime. You might have slight redness for a few hours. Makeup and normal activities are usually fine the same day. Many clients book a Hydrafacial a few days before an event for a fresh glow.

Light Chemical Peels

Light peels may cause mild flaking or redness for a day or two. Deeper peels have more downtime—we will advise based on the peel we recommend.

What Has More Downtime

Filler often involves a few days of swelling or bruising. Laser treatments and microneedling can cause redness or sensitivity for a few days. We will outline what to expect for any treatment we suggest so you can plan.

Planning Around an Event

If you have a set date, we recommend at least two weeks before for injectables so any bruising or swelling can resolve. For facials, a few days to a week before is often enough. We can help you choose a treatment and timing that fits your schedule.
