When to Book Treatment Before a Wedding or Event

Lumina Aesthetics — Raleigh, North Carolina

Introduction

Planning aesthetic treatments around a wedding, reunion, or photo shoot can feel overwhelming. Here’s a simple timeline to help you book with confidence at Lumina.

Injectables (Botox and Filler)

We recommend at least two weeks before your event—and many clients prefer three to four weeks. That allows time for any bruising or swelling to resolve. Filler in particular can cause swelling that peaks in the first few days and then settles. Do not book injectables the week of your event; you may not look your best in time.

Facials and Hydrafacial

A few days to a week before is usually ideal. Your skin will have time to calm and glow. Avoid booking the day before in case of rare sensitivity or redness that lasts a bit longer.

Laser and Microneedling

These can cause redness or peeling. We generally recommend at least one to two weeks before a major event so your skin has time to heal. Deeper peels or more aggressive settings may need more time—we will advise based on your treatment.

Start with a Consultation

If your event is months away, book a consultation early. We can map out a plan: what to do first, when to do it, and what to avoid in the final weeks. That way you are not rushing or guessing.

Summary

Injectables: at least two weeks before. Facials: a few days to a week before. Laser and microneedling: one to two weeks or more depending on treatment. When in doubt, book a consultation and we will give you a timeline that fits your date and your goals.
