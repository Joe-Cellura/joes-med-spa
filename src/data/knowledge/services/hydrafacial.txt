Lumina Aesthetics — Hydrafacial

Overview

Hydrafacial is a treatment that cleanses, exfoliates, extracts impurities, and hydrates the skin in one session. It uses a handheld device with different tips and serums. There is little to no downtime, and the treatment is often described as comfortable and relaxing. It is popular for a quick glow before events or as part of ongoing maintenance.

Who It's For

Clients who want cleaner, smoother, more hydrated skin with minimal downtime. It can benefit most skin types when performed by a trained provider. We customize the treatment to your skin; not every add-on or intensity is right for everyone.

Common Goals

Improve skin texture and glow, clear congestion, boost hydration, prepare skin before an event, maintain results between other treatments.

What Treatment Feels Like

The device passes over the skin with a gentle suction and serum flow. It may feel like a light vacuum or exfoliation. Most clients find it comfortable. Some areas may be more sensitive; we can adjust pressure or intensity.

Typical Appointment Length

30–45 minutes including consultation and any add-ons. Add-ons (e.g., boosters for brightening or acne) may extend the visit slightly.

Downtime

Minimal. You may have slight redness for a few hours. You can usually return to normal activities and makeup the same day. We will advise on sun protection and any product restrictions.

Results Timeline

Skin often looks smoother and more radiant immediately. Results are cumulative; many clients benefit from regular sessions (e.g., monthly or every few months) for maintenance.

Pricing Range

At Lumina, Hydrafacial is typically in the range of approximately $199–299 per session depending on add-ons and customization. We will quote your visit at booking or during check-in.

Consultation Guidance

We may ask about your skin goals, sensitivity, and any upcoming events. We will select the appropriate level and any boosters. No lengthy consultation is required for repeat clients who are familiar with the treatment.

Aftercare Notes

Use sunscreen and avoid harsh actives as we advise. Stay hydrated. We may recommend specific products to support results.

When This May Be a Good Fit

When you want a low-downtime treatment that delivers a quick glow. When you have an event and want to look refreshed a few days before. When you are building a maintenance routine.

When a Consultation Matters Most

First-time Hydrafacial, sensitive skin, or uncertainty about add-ons. We can recommend the right level and frequency.
