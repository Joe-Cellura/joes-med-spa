Lumina Aesthetics — Laser Hair Removal

Overview

Laser hair removal uses concentrated light to target hair follicles and reduce growth over time. It is not always permanent; many clients experience long-lasting reduction with maintenance sessions. At Lumina we use FDA-cleared devices and tailor settings to your skin type and hair color for safety and effectiveness.

Who It's For

Adults with unwanted hair in treatable areas (e.g., face, underarms, legs, bikini). Skin and hair color affect candidacy; laser works best on contrast between hair and skin. We assess your skin type and hair to determine if you are a good candidate and what to expect.

Common Goals

Reduce or remove hair in specific areas, simplify grooming, reduce ingrown hairs, achieve smoother skin long-term.

What Treatment Feels Like

The handpiece is applied to the skin; you may feel heat or a snapping sensation. We use cooling and can adjust settings for comfort. Treatment time depends on area size—small areas may be 15–20 minutes, larger areas longer.

Typical Appointment Length

15–45 minutes depending on area. Consultation and patch test (if needed) may add time at the first visit.

Downtime

Minimal. Skin may be slightly red or sensitive for a few hours. We will advise on sun avoidance and gentle care. Shaving is typically allowed between sessions; waxing and plucking are not, as they remove the follicle target.

Results Timeline

Hair may shed in the weeks after treatment. Multiple sessions are needed—typically 6–8 or more depending on area and growth cycle. Sessions are usually spaced 4–8 weeks apart. Results vary; many clients see significant reduction with maintenance sessions as needed.

Pricing Range

At Lumina, laser hair removal is often priced per area or as a package. Single sessions may start in the range of approximately $129–299+ depending on area size. Packages can offer better value. We will quote at your consultation.

Consultation Guidance

We review your skin type, hair color, medical history, and goals. We may do a patch test to check response and comfort. We explain how many sessions we expect and how to prepare (e.g., shaving before treatment, avoiding sun).

Aftercare Notes

Avoid sun exposure and use sunscreen as we advise. Do not wax or pluck between sessions. Shave if needed. We will give you written aftercare and schedule your next session.

When This May Be a Good Fit

When you want long-term hair reduction and have realistic expectations. When your skin and hair type are suitable and you can commit to a series.

When a Consultation Matters Most

First-time laser hair removal, darker skin tones, or history of skin sensitivity. We will ensure the right device and settings for your safety and results.
