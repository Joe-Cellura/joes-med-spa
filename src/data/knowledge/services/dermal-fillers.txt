Lumina Aesthetics — Dermal Fillers

Overview

Dermal fillers are injectable gels used to add volume, smooth lines, and enhance contour. Common areas include lips, cheeks, chin, jawline, and sometimes under the eyes (with careful technique and appropriate product). At Lumina we emphasize balance and proportion—fuller or more defined in a way that looks natural for your face.

Who It's For

Adults who want to restore volume, soften lines, or refine contour. Good candidates are generally in good health and have realistic expectations. We assess anatomy and goals; not every area or amount is right for every person. We do not treat pregnant or breastfeeding individuals or those with certain allergies or conditions.

Common Goals

Add volume to lips or cheeks, soften nasolabial folds, define chin or jawline, improve under-eye hollows (when appropriate), restore a more youthful contour while looking like oneself.

What Treatment Feels Like

Treatment involves injections with a needle or cannula. We may use topical numbing or product that contains lidocaine. Discomfort varies by area and technique. Swelling and tenderness are common afterward.

Typical Appointment Length

30–60 minutes depending on areas treated. Consultation and marking are included. First-time filler clients often need more time for discussion and planning.

Downtime

Swelling and possible bruising are common. Most swelling improves within a few days to a week; bruising can last a bit longer. We recommend avoiding strenuous exercise, alcohol, and blood thinners as advised. Some clients plan treatment when they can allow a few days of social downtime.

Results Timeline

You will see initial results right away, though swelling can make things look fuller at first. True result is usually visible once swelling subsides (about one to two weeks). Results typically last several months to a year or more depending on product and area.

Pricing Range

Filler is typically priced per syringe or per area. At Lumina, filler often starts in the range of approximately $750–1,200+ per syringe depending on product and area. Multiple areas or syringes will increase cost. We will give you an estimate at your consultation.

Consultation Guidance

We review your goals, anatomy, and any prior filler. We discuss which areas and how much product we recommend, and we explain risks (including swelling and bruising). We do not over-fill; we aim for natural proportion. Same-day treatment may be an option for some; for larger plans we may recommend a separate visit.

Aftercare Notes

Avoid rubbing or massaging the area. Avoid strenuous exercise, alcohol, and excessive heat for the time we specify. Sleep with head elevated if we recommend it. We provide written aftercare. Call us if you have unusual swelling, pain, or vision changes.

When This May Be a Good Fit

When you want volume or contour improvement and can accommodate a few days of possible swelling or bruising. When you have had a consultation and understand the plan and cost.

When a Consultation Matters Most

First-time filler, multiple areas, or concern about natural-looking results. We will recommend product, amount, and technique that fit your face and goals.
