Lumina Aesthetics — Microneedling

Overview

Microneedling uses fine needles to create controlled micro-channels in the skin. This can help improve texture, tone, and the appearance of acne scars, pores, and fine lines by supporting collagen and product absorption. At Lumina we use medical-grade devices and tailor depth and technique to your skin and goals.

Who It's For

Adults who want to improve skin texture, tone, acne scarring, pore appearance, or fine lines. Good candidates are generally in good health and can follow pre- and post-care. We do not perform microneedling on active infection, certain skin conditions, or pregnant clients.

Common Goals

Smooth texture, reduce the look of acne scars or pores, improve tone, soften fine lines, enhance product penetration when combined with serums.

What Treatment Feels Like

We typically use topical numbing before treatment. You may feel a vibrating or prickling sensation. Discomfort is usually manageable. We can adjust depth and speed for comfort while still achieving benefit.

Typical Appointment Length

45–60 minutes including numbing time, treatment, and aftercare. First-time clients may need a bit longer for consultation.

Downtime

Redness and sensitivity are expected for a few days. Skin may feel like a mild sunburn. We provide aftercare instructions; avoiding sun, harsh products, and makeup for the period we specify helps results and reduces risk. Some clients schedule when they can allow 24–48 hours of visible redness.

Results Timeline

Initial healing is within a few days. Collagen response builds over weeks. Many clients see gradual improvement in texture and tone. A series of treatments (e.g., 3–6 sessions spaced several weeks apart) is often recommended for best results with scarring or significant texture concerns.

Pricing Range

At Lumina, microneedling is typically in the range of approximately $399–599 per session depending on device, area, and any combined serums. Series pricing may be available. We will quote at your consultation.

Consultation Guidance

We assess your skin, goals, and tolerance. We explain depth, expected downtime, and how many sessions we recommend. We will also review pre-care (e.g., avoiding retinoids or sun) so your skin is prepared.

Aftercare Notes

Keep the skin clean and protected. Use the products we recommend; avoid retinoids, acids, and direct sun for the time we specify. Do not pick or scrub. We provide written instructions.

When This May Be a Good Fit

When you want to improve texture or scarring and can commit to aftercare and possibly a series. When you have no conflicting treatments or conditions.

When a Consultation Matters Most

First-time microneedling, history of keloids or poor healing, or significant scarring. We will recommend a plan that is safe and effective for you.
