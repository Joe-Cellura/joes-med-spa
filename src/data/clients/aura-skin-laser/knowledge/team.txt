Aura Skin & Laser Team

Dipna Patel, RN — Founder & Sole Practitioner

Dipna is a registered nurse and skincare expert with a passion for helping people of all skin tones look and feel their best. She founded Aura Skin & Laser with the goal of providing effective, personalized treatments in a boutique environment where every client feels known and cared for.

As the sole practitioner at Aura, every consultation and treatment is personally performed by Dipna. Clients work with the same expert at every visit — someone who knows their skin history, tracks their progress, and adjusts protocols as needed.

Prior to each treatment, Dipna takes time to listen to each client's concerns, answers their questions, and explains what to expect during and after the procedure. She ensures clients feel comfortable and confident throughout the process.

Dipna uses the Motus AZ+ laser platform and the Skinwave Aqua Facial system — both selected for their safety, effectiveness across all skin tones, and ability to deliver consistent results with minimal discomfort.

Special Focus

Dipna has particular expertise in treating clients with medium to dark skin tones — a population that has historically been underserved by the aesthetics industry due to limitations in older laser technology. The Motus AZ+ platform allows her to safely and effectively serve all skin tones with customized protocols.

Philosophy

Dipna believes that effective skin care should be personal. She does not rush treatments, does not oversell, and takes the time to set realistic expectations for every client. Her goal is for clients to see meaningful results and feel genuinely supported throughout their treatment journey.
