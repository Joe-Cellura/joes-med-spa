Your First Visit to Aura Skin & Laser

Your first visit begins with a complimentary consultation with Dipna Patel, RN. Dipna will:

- Review your skin type, tone, and specific concerns
- Discuss your treatment goals
- Explain which services are appropriate for your skin
- Walk you through realistic expectations for results and timeline
- Provide transparent pricing before any treatment begins

Because Dipna is the sole practitioner, every client receives her personal attention from the very first visit. She takes the time to understand your unique skin needs and designs a protocol specifically for you.

Skin Assessment

Dipna will assess your skin tone, hair characteristics (for laser hair reduction), and any relevant skin history before recommending a treatment plan. She will explain how the technology works, what to expect during and after treatment, and answer any questions you have.

Treatment Plans

Treatment plans are personalized based on your skin type, goals, and lifestyle. Dipna will explain options clearly — including single sessions and package pricing — and will never recommend more than what is appropriate for your situation.

Same-Day Treatment

Same-day treatment may be available following a consultation if Dipna determines it is appropriate and you are ready to proceed. For some treatments, preparation requirements (such as shaving for laser hair reduction) must be met in advance.

No Pressure

There is no obligation to begin treatment the same day. Many clients prefer to review their treatment plan at home before scheduling their first session. Dipna is available for follow-up questions after your consultation.
