Booking at Aura Skin & Laser

Clients can request appointments online through the booking page or by calling (919) 205-3693. The booking page is the easiest way to choose a time that works.

Aura is appointment-only. Same-day appointments may be available depending on Dipna's schedule — call to check.

New Client Consultations

New clients are encouraged to start with a free consultation. During the consultation, Dipna will assess your skin, discuss your goals, and recommend a personalized treatment plan with transparent pricing. There is no obligation to begin treatment the same day.

Preparing for Laser Hair Reduction

- Shave the treatment area 24 hours before your appointment
- Do not wax, tweeze, or thread for 4 weeks prior to treatment
- Avoid sun exposure and tanning before your session
- Arrive with clean, product-free skin in the treatment area

Preparing for Skin Treatments (Revitalization, Dark Spots, Vascular)

- Avoid direct sun exposure and sunburn for at least 2 weeks before treatment
- Avoid retinols and exfoliants for 3 to 5 days before your appointment
- Arrive with clean skin — no makeup or heavy moisturizers

Preparing for Skinwave Aqua Facial

- Avoid retinols and exfoliants 3 to 5 days before
- Arrive with clean skin

Event Timing

For the Skinwave Aqua Facial, book 3 to 5 days before your event to allow any mild redness to settle while keeping your skin at its best. For laser treatments, avoid scheduling close to major events as some redness or sensitivity is normal for 24 to 48 hours post-treatment.
