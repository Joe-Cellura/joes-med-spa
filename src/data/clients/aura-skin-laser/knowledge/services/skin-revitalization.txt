Skin Revitalization at Aura Skin & Laser

Technology: Motus AZ+ (Moveo SR)

The Moveo SR treatment uses non-ablative laser technology to improve skin texture, tone, and radiance. It stimulates collagen production and promotes cell renewal without ablating the skin surface.

Results include reduced signs of aging, improved skin elasticity, diminished appearance of acne scars or blemishes, and overall skin rejuvenation with a more even, refreshed complexion.

Who It's For

Clients with dull or uneven skin tone, fine lines, mild texture concerns, or signs of aging. Also appropriate for clients managing mild acne scarring or looking to improve overall skin quality.

What Treatment Feels Like

The Moveo SR uses a gentle sweeping motion. Most clients experience mild warmth during the procedure. Some redness is normal immediately after treatment and typically resolves within 24 to 48 hours.

Typical Appointment Length

30 to 45 minutes.

Downtime

Minimal. Mild redness for 24 to 48 hours. Most clients return to normal activities the same day.

Results Timeline

Some clients notice an improvement in glow and texture after a single session. Collagen-building effects develop over 4 to 8 weeks. A series of 3 sessions is recommended for optimal results.

Pricing

- Non-ablative laser facial: $350 per session / $945 for 3 sessions
- Tone & texture treatment: $450 per session / $1,215 for 3 sessions

Aftercare

Wear SPF 30 or higher daily. Avoid direct sun exposure for one week after treatment. Avoid retinols and exfoliants for 5 days post-treatment.
