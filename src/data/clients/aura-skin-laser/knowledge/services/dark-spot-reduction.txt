Dark Spot and Pigmentation Reduction at Aura Skin & Laser

Technology: Motus AZ+ (Moveo PL)

The Moveo PL treatment targets melanin to reduce the appearance of sunspots, age spots, post-inflammatory pigmentation, and sun damage. The laser selectively heats and breaks down pigmented cells, which are then naturally cleared by the body over time.

Who It's For

Clients with sunspots, age spots, freckles, post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation (PIH), or general sun damage on the face, chest, arms, back, or legs.

What Treatment Feels Like

A quick, mild snapping or warmth sensation during treatment. Most clients tolerate it well. Treated spots may temporarily darken and develop a slight crust before fading — this is normal and a sign the treatment is working.

Typical Appointment Length

30 to 45 minutes.

Downtime

Treated areas may appear darker for 7 to 14 days before the pigment flakes off. Avoid picking or scrubbing the area during this time. Sun protection is essential.

Results Timeline

Results can often be seen after the first session as pigment clears over 2 to 4 weeks. Multiple sessions are recommended for more extensive or stubborn pigmentation.
