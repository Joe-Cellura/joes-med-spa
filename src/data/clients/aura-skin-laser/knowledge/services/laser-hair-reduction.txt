Laser Hair Reduction at Aura Skin & Laser

Technology: Motus AZ+ (Moveo HR)

Aura offers laser hair reduction using the Motus AZ+ laser platform. The Moveo HR treatment is safe and effective for all skin tones, including darker complexions.

Sessions are fast and comfortable. The Motus AZ+ uses a unique sweeping motion rather than individual pulses, reducing overall treatment time and minimizing discomfort. Most clients describe the sensation as mild warmth.

Who It's For

Anyone looking for long-lasting hair reduction in any treatment area. Particularly well-suited for clients with darker skin tones who have been turned away from laser hair removal in the past.

Preparation

- Shave the treatment area 24 hours before your appointment
- Do not wax, tweeze, or thread for 4 weeks prior
- Avoid sun exposure and tanning before your session
- Arrive with clean skin, free of lotion or deodorant in the treatment area

Number of Sessions

Most clients need 6 to 12 sessions for optimal results. Hair grows in cycles, and laser is most effective during the active growth phase. Many clients notice smoother, slower-growing hair after 1 to 3 sessions.

Aftercare

- Avoid sun exposure for at least one week after treatment
- Wear SPF 30 or higher daily
- Avoid heat, strenuous exercise, and hot showers for 24 hours
- Do not wax or tweeze between sessions — shaving is fine
