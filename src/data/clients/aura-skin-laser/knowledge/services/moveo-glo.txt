Moveo GLO at Aura Skin & Laser

Technology: Motus AZ+ (Moveo PL + SR + VL combined)

Moveo GLO is a 3-in-1 combination treatment that addresses pigmentation, skin revitalization, and vascular concerns in a single session. It combines the Moveo PL (pigmentation), Moveo SR (skin revitalization), and Moveo VL (vascular) treatments.

Who It's For

Clients looking for comprehensive skin improvement with fewer visits. Moveo GLO is ideal for clients who have multiple concerns — such as sun damage, uneven tone, redness, and aging — and want to address them efficiently.

What Treatment Feels Like

The same mild warmth or snapping sensation as the individual treatments. Because it combines three modalities, the session is longer than a single treatment but well tolerated by most clients.

Typical Appointment Length

45 to 60 minutes.

Downtime

Similar to individual treatments — some redness and temporary darkening of pigmented spots for 7 to 14 days. Full recovery between 1 to 2 weeks depending on the areas treated and individual response.

Results Timeline

Improvements develop over 4 to 8 weeks as the skin responds to all three laser modalities. Most clients see meaningful change after a single session, with continued improvement after 3 sessions.

Aftercare

Strict sun avoidance and SPF 50 daily. Avoid retinols and exfoliants for 5 to 7 days post-treatment. Avoid heat and strenuous exercise for 24 to 48 hours.
