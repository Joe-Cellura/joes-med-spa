Skinwave Aqua Facial at Aura Skin & Laser

Technology: Skinwave

The Skinwave Aqua Facial cleanses, exfoliates, and hydrates the skin in one session. It uses aqueous solutions and gentle suction to remove impurities while delivering antioxidants and hydrating serums into the skin. A contouring roller helps stimulate lymphatic circulation and relax the skin surface.

Who It's For

All skin types, including oily, dry, sensitive, acne-prone, and combination. The Skinwave is a popular choice before events for clients who want an immediate glow without the risk of downtime from more intensive treatments.

What Treatment Feels Like

Gentle and relaxing. The treatment involves suction and roller movements across the skin that most clients find comfortable. There is no heat or pain involved.

Typical Appointment Length

45 to 60 minutes.

Downtime

None to minimal. Skin may feel slightly tight or appear mildly flushed for up to 72 hours. Most clients look refreshed and glowing immediately after the session.

Results Timeline

Results are visible immediately. For best ongoing results, monthly maintenance sessions are recommended.

Event Timing

For event prep, book the Skinwave 3 to 5 days before your event. This allows any mild redness to fully settle while keeping your skin at its most hydrated and radiant.

Preparation

Avoid retinols and exfoliants 3 to 5 days before treatment. Arrive with clean skin — no makeup or heavy moisturizers.

Aftercare

Wear SPF 50 daily. Avoid retinols and exfoliants for 5 days post-treatment. Avoid direct sun exposure for one week.
