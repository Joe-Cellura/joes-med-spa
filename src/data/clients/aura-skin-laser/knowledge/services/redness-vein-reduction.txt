Redness and Vein Reduction at Aura Skin & Laser

Technology: Motus AZ+ (Moveo VL)

The Moveo VL treatment targets vascular concerns including diffuse facial redness, spider veins, and superficial facial veins. The laser heats and collapses the targeted vessels, which are then gradually absorbed by the body.

Who It's For

Clients with diffuse facial redness, visible spider veins on the face or legs, or superficial broken capillaries. Also appropriate for clients with rosacea-related redness (general flushing or visible vessels).

What Treatment Feels Like

A mild snapping or warmth sensation during treatment. The area may appear temporarily redder immediately after the session — this typically resolves within 24 to 72 hours.

Typical Appointment Length

30 to 45 minutes.

Downtime

24 to 72 hours of mild redness or flushing in the treated area. Most clients return to normal activity the same day. Avoid heat, exercise, and direct sun exposure for 24 to 48 hours.

Results Timeline

Treated vessels go through changes over approximately 8 weeks. Most clients see noticeable improvement after 2 to 3 sessions.

Aftercare
