Title: Everything You Need to Know About the Skinwave Aqua Facial

The Skinwave Aqua Facial combines deep cleansing, exfoliation, and hydration in a single session. It delivers antioxidants, removes impurities, and uses a contouring roller to stimulate lymphatic circulation.

It works for oily, dry, acne-prone, and sensitive skin. It is a popular choice before events because it delivers an immediate glow with no downtime.

How Often

Once a month for maintenance. Book 3 to 5 days before an event.

After Treatment

Skin may feel tight or appear mildly flushed for up to 72 hours. Wear SPF 50 daily. Avoid retinols and exfoliants for 5 days post-treatment.
