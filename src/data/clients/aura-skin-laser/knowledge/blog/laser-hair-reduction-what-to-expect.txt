Title: Laser Hair Reduction: What to Expect at Your First Session

Laser hair reduction has come a long way. At Aura, we use the Motus AZ+ laser, which is designed to be effective for all skin tones with minimal discomfort.

Preparation

Shave the area 24 hours before your appointment. Do not wax, tweeze, or thread for at least four weeks prior. Avoid tanning or significant sun exposure before your session.

During the Session

Dipna will review your skin type and hair characteristics before beginning. The Motus AZ+ uses a sweeping motion rather than individual pulses, making it faster and more comfortable than traditional lasers. Most clients describe the sensation as mild warmth.

Number of Sessions

Most clients need 6 to 12 sessions for optimal results. You may start seeing smoother, slower-growing hair after 1 to 3 sessions.

Aftercare

Avoid sun exposure for at least a week. Wear SPF daily. Avoid heat and strenuous exercise for 24 hours post-treatment.

If you have questions specific to your skin or hair type, the best next step is a free consultation with Dipna.
