Title: Why Laser Treatments Work for All Skin Tones at Aura

For years, people with medium to dark skin tones were told that laser treatments were not safe for them. Older technology had difficulty distinguishing between melanin in hair and melanin in skin.

The Motus AZ+ laser platform uses Alexandrite and Nd:YAG wavelengths calibrated for different skin tones. It targets the hair follicle while minimizing risk to surrounding skin, making it one of the safest options for darker complexions.

If you have previously been turned away from laser treatments or had a poor experience elsewhere, a consultation with Dipna is worth having. She will assess your skin type, explain realistic expectations, and design a protocol right for you.

All skin types are welcome at Aura. That is not a marketing line. It is how the practice was built.
