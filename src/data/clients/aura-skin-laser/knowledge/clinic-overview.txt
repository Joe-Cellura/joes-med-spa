Technology

Aura uses the Motus AZ+ laser platform, which is safe and effective for all skin tones including darker complexions. The system uses Alexandrite and Nd:YAG wavelengths and is designed to minimize discomfort while maximizing results.

The Skinwave Aqua Facial system delivers deep cleansing, exfoliation, and hydration in a single session and is suitable for all skin types.

History and Background

Aura Skin & Laser was established in 2023 by Dipna Patel, a registered nurse and skincare expert based in Cary, North Carolina. Dipna founded the practice with a clear mission: to provide effective, personalized skin and laser treatments for all skin tones in a boutique, comfortable environment. As the sole practitioner, Dipna performs every treatment herself, ensuring each client receives consistent, expert care from someone who knows their skin.
