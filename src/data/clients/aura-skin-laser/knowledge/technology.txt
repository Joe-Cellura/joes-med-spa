Aura Skin & Laser Technology

Primary Platform: Motus AZ+ by Cartessa Aesthetics

The Motus AZ+ is the core laser platform used at Aura Skin & Laser. It combines two wavelengths — Alexandrite and Nd:YAG — to treat a wide range of skin concerns safely and effectively across all skin tones.

Key differentiators:
- Safe and effective for all skin tones including darker complexions that were historically difficult to treat with older laser technology
- Uses a sweeping Moveo motion rather than individual pulses, reducing treatment time and minimizing discomfort significantly
- Most clients describe the sensation as mild warmth rather than the sharp snapping sensation of older systems
- Versatile platform that handles multiple treatment categories with different applicators

Treatment applications:
- Moveo HR: Laser hair reduction
- Moveo PL: Dark spot and pigmentation reduction
- Moveo SR: Skin revitalization and texture improvement
- Moveo VL: Redness and vascular reduction
- Moveo GLO: 3-in-1 combination of PL, SR, and VL

Skin tone compatibility:
The Motus AZ+ is specifically designed to treat all skin tones safely. The Nd:YAG wavelength allows for effective treatment of darker complexions that were previously excluded from laser procedures. Dipna customizes treatment settings to each client's individual skin profile.

Pain and comfort:
The Motus AZ+ is marketed as a virtually pain-free laser system. The Moveo technology moves the laser energy across the skin in a continuous sweeping motion, distributing heat more evenly and reducing peak energy at any single point — which is what makes traditional lasers uncomfortable.

Also used at Aura:
Skinwave by Envy Medical — an aqua facial device that combines cleansing, exfoliation, hydration, and antioxidant infusion in one treatment. The Skinwave also includes a contouring roller that stimulates lymphatic circulation.
