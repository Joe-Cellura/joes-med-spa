MedSpa 501 Overview

MedSpa 501 is an award-winning medical spa located in Chapel Hill, North Carolina, serving Chapel Hill, Fearrington, Briar Chapel, and Pittsboro. The practice blends cutting-edge aesthetic treatments with functional wellness in a modern, welcoming environment.

Address: 69 Knox Way, Suite 108, Chapel Hill, NC 27516
Phone: (984) 336-0501
Email: hello@medspa501.com
Hours: Monday through Friday 9am to 6pm. Saturday and Sunday closed.

MedSpa 501 was voted Best Spa and Best New Business by Chapel Hill Magazine in 2025. Certifications include the Institute for Functional Medicine and 3x4 Genetics. The practice has a unique partnership with 501 Pharmacy for custom IV formulations, compounded cosmeceuticals, and AI-powered skin assessments.

Services include Botox, Xeomin, Daxxify, dermal fillers, IV nutrient therapy, medical weight loss (GLP-1), HydraFacials, microneedling (SkinStylus), dermaplaning, chemical peels, PRF, peptide therapy, permanent makeup, paramedical tattoos, medical grade skincare, Glass Skin Micro-tox, and functional wellness.

Pricing: MedSpa 501 publishes pricing at medspa501.com/pricing. Clients are encouraged to visit the pricing page or book a consultation for a personalized quote. Pricing varies by treatment type, provider, and individual goals.

Payment Plans: MedSpa 501 offers payment plans. Contact the practice for details at (984) 336-0501 or hello@medspa501.com.
