PRF Services at MedSpa 501 — Detailed

MedSpa 501 offers three PRF-based treatments:

1. EZ Gel PRF Filler ($950 per session, $850 for 2nd session within 60 days)
An advanced bio-stimulating treatment that adds volume, enhances skin quality, and improves elasticity using the client's own platelet-rich fibrin as a natural filler alternative.

2. EZ PRF ($850 per session)
The liquid form of PRF. Injected to stimulate tissue regeneration, collagen production, and skin rejuvenation without adding volume. Often used for under-eye hollows, fine lines, and overall skin quality.

3. Microneedling with PRF ($550 per session)
Combines the collagen induction benefits of microneedling with the regenerative power of PRF. PRF is applied topically and microneedling is used to drive it into the skin layers where it releases growth factors and boosts collagen.

PRF vs PRP: PRF uses a slower centrifuge spin than PRP, preserving more platelets, white blood cells, and stem cells. The fibrin matrix releases growth factors more slowly for longer-lasting results. PRF contains no additives — 100% natural.
