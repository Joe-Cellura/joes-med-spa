IV Nutrient Therapy at MedSpa 501

IV nutrient therapy delivers vitamins, minerals, and hydration directly into the bloodstream for full absorption — bypassing the digestive system entirely. MedSpa 501 IV formulations are custom-designed in partnership with 501 Pharmacy.

Common uses: increased energy, improved hydration, immune support, faster recovery after illness or travel, and general wellness optimization.

All IV treatments are administered by licensed nursing staff in a supervised clinical setting. No significant pre-care required — stay hydrated before your appointment.
