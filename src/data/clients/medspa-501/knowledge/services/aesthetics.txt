Aesthetics at MedSpa 501

HydraFacial: Cleanses, exfoliates, extracts, and hydrates the skin in a single session. Suitable for all skin types. Results include immediate glow with no downtime.

SkinStylus Microneedling: Creates micro-channels in the skin to stimulate collagen production and improve texture, tone, and overall skin quality. Pre-care: avoid retinoids and exfoliants for 5 to 7 days. Post-care: skin will be red and sensitive for 24 to 72 hours, avoid sun exposure.

Dermaplaning: Physical exfoliation using a surgical blade to remove dead skin cells and peach fuzz. Results in smoother skin and better product absorption.

Chemical Peels: Medical-grade peels to address pigmentation, texture, fine lines, and radiance. Post-care: skin will peel for 3 to 7 days, avoid picking, wear SPF.

Glass Skin Micro-tox: Micro-injections of diluted neuromodulator into the skin surface (not into muscles) to reduce pore size, control oil production, and improve overall skin texture and radiance. Results in smoother, more refined skin appearance.
