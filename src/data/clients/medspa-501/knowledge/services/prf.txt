PRF at MedSpa 501

PRF (Platelet-Rich Fibrin) uses a small sample of the client's own blood. The blood is processed in a centrifuge to concentrate platelets and growth factors, which are then applied or injected into treatment areas.

PRF stimulates collagen production, improves skin texture and quality, reduces under-eye hollows, and supports hair restoration. Because it uses the client's own biology, there is no risk of allergic reaction.

PRF is different from PRP in that it uses a slower centrifuge speed, preserving more cells and creating a fibrin matrix that releases growth factors more slowly for longer-lasting benefits.

Sessions typically take 45 to 60 minutes. A series of treatments is recommended. Progressive improvement occurs over several weeks as collagen production increases.
