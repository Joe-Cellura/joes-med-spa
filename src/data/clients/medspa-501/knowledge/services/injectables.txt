Injectables at MedSpa 501

Neuromodulators: Botox, Xeomin, and Daxxify.

All three temporarily relax the muscles causing dynamic wrinkles including forehead lines, frown lines between the brows (11s), and crow's feet around the eyes. Results are natural and refreshed, not frozen.

Botox and Xeomin results typically last 3 to 4 months. Daxxify results typically last 6 months or more due to its peptide-based formulation.

Pre-care: Avoid blood-thinning medications for 7 days prior. Arrive with a clean face.
Post-care: Do not massage treated areas for 24 hours. Do not lie down for 4 hours. Avoid strenuous exercise the day of treatment. Full results visible in 2 to 3 weeks.

Dermal fillers restore lost volume, smooth static lines, and enhance features including lips, cheeks, jawline, and under-eyes. Results are immediate and last 6 to 18 months depending on the product and area.

All injectable treatments are performed by licensed providers with a conservative, artful approach.

DETAILED INJECTABLE FAQ

Areas that can be treated with Botox, Xeomin, and Daxxify:
- Glabella / 11 lines between the brows
- Frontalis / forehead lines
- Crow's feet
- Nasalis / bunny lines
- Masseters (jaw muscle — facial slimming, TMJ pain)
- Smoker's lines around the lips
- Lip flip
- DAO / frown lines
- Mentalis / chin orange peel appearance
- Platysmal neck bands
- Hyperhidrosis / excessive sweating underarms
- Other areas based on individual need

Onset of results:
- Botox and Xeomin: visible results within 5 to 10 days, full effects at 3 weeks
- Daxxify: results start within 1 to 2 days, full effects at 3 weeks

Duration of results:
- Botox and Xeomin: typically 2 to 4 months
- Daxxify: typically up to 6 months at higher dosing for some clients

Discomfort: The needle used is very tiny. Most clients describe the sensation as a small snap or pinch. An ice pack can be applied before treatment.

Combining treatments: Injectables can be combined with dermal fillers, PRF, microneedling, and other treatments on the same day.

Unhappy with results: If desired results are not seen 3 weeks after treatment, a refinement appointment can be scheduled no sooner than 2 weeks and no later than 3.5 weeks post-treatment. Fees based on product used.

DERMAL FILLER DETAILS

Filler brands available: Radiesse, HyperDilute Radiesse, RHA collection (Redensity, RHA 2, RHA 3, RHA 4), Restylane collection (Lyft, Contour, Kysse, and more), EZ Gel PRF bio-filler.

Amount per syringe: typically 1 to 1.2mL. MedSpa 501 takes a low and slow approach, starting conservative to assess results after swelling subsides.

HyperDilute Radiesse: Radiesse diluted with saline and lidocaine, injected superficially as a collagen stimulating agent. Improves skin texture, volume, and wrinkles. Results develop at 3 to 4 weeks and continue for up to 6 months. Recommended 2 to 3 treatments spaced 1 to 2 months apart. Annual maintenance recommended.

RHA Fillers: Resilient hyaluronic acid fillers designed to move with facial expressions. Results last up to 15 months in wrinkles and folds, 1 year in lipstick lines.

Non-Surgical BBL: Uses biostimulatory fillers (Radiesse) to enhance the buttocks non-surgically. A $100 consultation is required prior to booking. Options range from 10 to 40 syringes.
