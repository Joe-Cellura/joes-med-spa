MedSpa 501 — Pre and Post Care Instructions

NEUROTOXIN (Botox, Xeomin, Daxxify):
Before treatment: Avoid blood-thinning medications (vitamin E, aspirin, NSAIDs) for 7 days prior. Schedule at least 4 weeks before a special event. Arrive with a clean face, no makeup.
After treatment: Do not massage or manipulate injected areas for 24 hours. Do not lie down for 4 hours after treatment. Avoid strenuous exercise for the remainder of the day. Results take 2 to 3 weeks to fully develop and last 2 to 4 months depending on area and product.

DERMAL FILLERS:
Before treatment: Avoid blood-thinning medications for 7 days prior. Avoid alcohol 24 hours before. Schedule at least 4 weeks before a special event.
After treatment: Avoid touching or massaging treated areas for 24 hours. Avoid strenuous exercise and excessive heat for 24 hours. Some swelling and bruising is normal and typically resolves within 7 to 14 days.

HYDRAFACIAL:
Before treatment: Arrive with a clean face. Avoid retinoids or exfoliants for 48 hours prior.
After treatment: Skin may appear slightly flushed for a few hours. Avoid direct sun exposure and wear SPF. Avoid heavy makeup for the remainder of the day.

SKINSTYLUS MICRONEEDLING:
Before treatment: Avoid retinoids, exfoliants, and blood-thinning supplements for 5 to 7 days prior. No active breakouts or skin infections in the treatment area.
After treatment: Skin will appear red and feel sensitive for 24 to 72 hours. Keep skin clean and moisturized. Avoid sun exposure and wear SPF daily. Avoid makeup for 24 hours.

CHEMICAL PEELS:
Before treatment: Avoid retinoids and exfoliants for 5 to 7 days prior. Avoid waxing or laser treatments in the treatment area.
After treatment: Skin will peel and flake for 3 to 7 days. Do not pick or peel skin manually. Moisturize generously. Avoid sun exposure and wear SPF.

IV HYDRATION:
No specific pre-care required. Stay hydrated before your appointment. After treatment: Continue to stay well-hydrated. Side effects are rare but may include mild bruising at the IV site.
