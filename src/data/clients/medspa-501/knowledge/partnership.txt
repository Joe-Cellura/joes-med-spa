MedSpa 501 and 501 Pharmacy Partnership

MedSpa 501 has a unique integrated partnership with 501 Pharmacy in Chapel Hill to provide clients with customized nutrition and skincare solutions.

Partnership benefits:
1. Custom Compounded Cosmeceuticals — MedSpa 501 providers perform individualized skin assessments and work with 501 Pharmacy pharmacists to compound a custom cosmeceutical prescription made specifically for each client.
2. Pharmacist-Designed IV Nutrient Therapies — IV formulations at MedSpa 501 were co-developed with 501 Pharmacy pharmacists for high quality and effectiveness.
3. AI-Powered Rapid Skin Assessment — Available on-demand at 501 Pharmacy. Clients can access a quick facial scan to analyze their skin and identify customized solutions.

501 Pharmacy is a nine-time Best of Chapel Hill winner. Pharmacists include Jennifer Vassie, PharmD (PCCA certified, Bioidentical Hormone Replacement Therapy specialist) and Dr. Han Su, PharmD (co-founder, certified immunizer).

Any custom compounded cosmeceuticals or medications can be delivered directly to the client's home.
