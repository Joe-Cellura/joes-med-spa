Frequently Asked Questions — MedSpa 501

Q: What neuromodulators do you offer?
A: Botox, Xeomin, and Daxxify. Daxxify lasts 6+ months. Botox and Xeomin last 3 to 4 months.

Q: Do you offer memberships?
A: Yes. The Glow Collective includes GlowTox ($2,200/year, quarterly neuromodulator treatments) and SkinGlow ($2,000/year, monthly skin treatments). Both require a 3-month minimum.

Q: Do you offer medical weight loss?
A: Yes. GLP-1 based programs with ongoing provider supervision.

Q: What is IV nutrient therapy?
A: Custom vitamin and hydration drips delivered into the bloodstream, formulated in partnership with 501 Pharmacy.

Q: What is PRF?
A: Platelet-Rich Fibrin — a regenerative treatment using your own growth factors to improve skin and support hair restoration.

Q: What is Glass Skin Micro-tox?
A: Micro-injections of diluted neuromodulator into the skin surface to reduce pore size, oil production, and improve skin texture and radiance — without muscle relaxation.

Q: What is the 501 Pharmacy partnership?
A: MedSpa 501 partners with 501 Pharmacy to offer custom compounded cosmeceuticals, pharmacist-designed IV formulations, and an AI-powered skin assessment tool.
