Functional Medicine and Root-Cause Wellness

MedSpa 501 incorporates functional wellness concepts that focus on identifying and addressing root causes of symptoms.

Rather than only treating symptoms, this approach may evaluate:
- Hormones
- Metabolism
- Nutrition
- Inflammation
- Lifestyle factors

Clients may seek this approach for concerns such as fatigue, weight changes, or long-term wellness goals.

This aligns with a more personalized and preventative model of care often referred to as Medicine 3.0.
