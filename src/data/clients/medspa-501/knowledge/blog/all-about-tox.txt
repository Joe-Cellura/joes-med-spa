All About Tox: Comparing Botox, Xeomin, and Daxxify

MedSpa 501 educates clients on three popular neuromodulators: Botox, Xeomin, and Daxxify.

These treatments are used to relax targeted muscles and soften dynamic wrinkles caused by repeated facial movement.

Common treatment areas may include:
- Forehead lines
- Frown lines / 11 lines
- Crow's feet
- Bunny lines
- Lip flip area
- Chin
- Neck bands
- Masseters in some cases
- Underarm sweating in some cases

Timing guidance used by MedSpa 501:
- Botox and Xeomin: visible results often begin within 5 to 10 days, with full effect around 3 weeks
- Daxxify: results may begin in 1 to 2 days, with full effect around 3 weeks

Typical duration guidance:
- Botox and Xeomin: about 2 to 4 months
- Daxxify: may last longer for some clients, sometimes up to 6 months at higher dosing

Selection depends on anatomy, treatment goals, prior experience, desired longevity, and provider recommendation.
