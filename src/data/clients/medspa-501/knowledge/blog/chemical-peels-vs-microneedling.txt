Chemical Peels vs Microneedling

MedSpa 501 helps clients compare chemical peels and microneedling based on the type of skin concern they want to improve.

Chemical peels:
- Focus on exfoliation and surface renewal
- May help with dullness, acne marks, pigmentation, and texture
- Can be a strong option for clients focused on tone and superficial skin refinement

Microneedling:
- Focuses on collagen stimulation
- May help with texture, fine lines, acne scarring, and overall skin rejuvenation
- Can be a better fit when deeper collagen support is the priority

The right treatment depends on the client's skin condition, goals, downtime preferences, and provider recommendation.

Some clients may benefit more from one treatment than the other, while others may use both at different times as part of a broader skin plan.
