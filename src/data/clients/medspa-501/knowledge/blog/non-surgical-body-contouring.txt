Non-Surgical Hip and Booty Contouring

MedSpa 501 offers non-surgical body contouring using injectable techniques designed to enhance shape and volume without surgery.

This approach may be used to create a fuller or more contoured appearance using filler-based methods.

Options referenced in pricing include tiered treatment plans based on the number of syringes used.

Treatment is personalized and requires consultation to determine candidacy, goals, and the appropriate treatment plan.
