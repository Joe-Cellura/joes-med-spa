Lip Flip vs Lip Filler

MedSpa 501 helps clients understand that lip flip and lip filler are not the same treatment.

Lip Flip:
- Uses a neuromodulator such as Botox, Xeomin, or Daxxify
- Relaxes the muscle around the upper lip
- Creates a subtle outward roll of the lip
- Works best for clients who want minimal enhancement and little to no added volume

Lip Filler:
- Uses filler to add volume, shape, and structure
- Can improve lip definition and fullness
- Usually creates a more noticeable change than a lip flip
- Can be tailored conservatively or more dramatically depending on goals

Some clients may be better suited for one option, while others may benefit from a combination approach depending on anatomy and aesthetic goals.
