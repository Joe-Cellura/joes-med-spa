MedSpa 501 — Payment Plans (Cherry Financing)

MedSpa 501 offers payment plans through Cherry, a buy now, pay later company for health and wellness purchases.

Core financing highlights:
- No hard credit checks, ever
- True 0% APR options available
- Interest-bearing plans with APRs as low as 5.99%
- Up to $50,000 approvals
- No hidden fees
- Application takes about 60 seconds
- Approvals are valid for 30 days
- No prepayment penalties

How Cherry works:
1. See if you qualify — the application takes about 60 seconds
2. Get care — use your approval to pay for treatment
3. Pay over time — choose a plan length that fits your needs, including 0% APR options for eligible clients

Important details:
- 0% APR and other promotional rates are subject to eligibility
- Exact terms and APR depend on credit score and other factors
- Once the purchase price is entered, available down payment options are shown
- The down payment is due when the provider confirms funds are needed to move forward with services
- Clients should bring a bank-issued debit card or credit card when ready for checkout
- Making payments via credit card results in a 2.99% processing fee
- There is no processing fee for making a down payment with a traditional bank-issued debit card

Approval / account details:
- Approvals are valid for 30 days
- If eligible, approval increases may be requested through the Cherry Consumer Portal
- Increase decisions are subject to eligibility
- If a client does not see the option to request an increase, they are not eligible at that time

Early payoff:
- Cherry does not charge prepayment penalties
- Paying early may reduce some interest costs on interest-bearing plans

0% APR offer details:
- If a client is approved for a 0% APR offer, that promotion does not expire during the current approval term as long as the account remains current

Refunds:
- Clients must contact MedSpa 501 directly to request a refund
- Cherry cannot initiate a refund without MedSpa 501 approval
- Once MedSpa 501 authorizes a refund, Cherry deposits the refunded amount into the account and adjusts account status and loan balance accordingly
- Partial refunds reduce the remaining balance
- Full refunds return any payments already made on the loan

Credit reporting:
- Cherry may report borrower account status to Equifax
- Cherry may report late payments, missed payments, and other delinquencies to the credit bureaus
- Missing payments may affect credit

Positioning guidance for assistant use:
Payment plans are available for clients who want to move forward with treatment now and pay over time. Financing is handled through Cherry, and exact eligibility and terms depend on the client's approval.
