How to Book an Appointment

Patients can request a consultation or appointment online at the booking page or by calling (704) 555-0192. The booking page is the easiest way to choose a time that works for you.

Consultations

Consultations are available for new patients and are the best starting point for anyone considering a new treatment. During a consultation, the dentist will review your oral health, listen to your goals, and provide a clear treatment plan with transparent pricing. There is no obligation to start treatment at the same visit.

Same-Day Availability

Same-day appointments are sometimes available for cleanings and routine procedures. For dental emergencies involving pain, broken teeth, or lost fillings, we reserve time each day for urgent needs — call us as early as possible.

Planning Ahead

For cosmetic treatments like veneers, Invisalign, or implants, it is best to book a consultation 2 to 4 weeks before you want to begin. These treatments involve multiple visits and a personalized plan that takes time to develop.

If you have a specific event like a wedding or reunion where you want your smile looking its best, reach out early. Whitening can be done fairly close to an event, but veneers and orthodontics require more lead time.

Insurance and Pricing

We work with most major dental insurance plans. When you book, we can verify your coverage in advance and let you know what to expect. For patients without insurance, we provide a clear fee schedule and offer payment options.

Follow-Up Care

After treatment, follow-up visits may be recommended depending on the procedure. Our team will walk you through what to expect before you leave and reach out to check in after more involved treatments.
