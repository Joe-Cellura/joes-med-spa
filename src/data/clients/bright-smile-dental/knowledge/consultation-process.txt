Your First Visit

Your first visit to Bright Smile Dental begins with a thorough oral exam. Depending on when you last saw a dentist and what your goals are, we may take X-rays to get a full picture of your oral health.

You will meet with Dr. Park or Dr. Patel, who will review your oral health history, listen to what you want to improve, and explain all relevant treatment options clearly.

What We Assess

- Current oral health: teeth, gums, bite, and existing dental work
- Hygiene habits and home care
- Areas of concern: cavities, worn enamel, gum recession, or other issues
- Cosmetic goals: whitening, alignment, shape, or smile transformation

Treatment Planning

After the exam, your provider will walk through a personalized treatment plan with you. This includes what is recommended, why, what it involves, and what it costs. If multiple treatments are relevant, they will explain the order and timing.

There is never pressure to begin treatment the same day. Some patients want to think it over or consult with a partner before deciding. That is completely fine.

Pricing Transparency

All treatment plans include clear pricing before any work begins. We will also review your insurance coverage and explain what is likely to be covered versus out-of-pocket.

Same-Day Treatment

For simple procedures like cleanings or basic restorations, same-day treatment may be possible if you are ready and time permits. For cosmetic work like veneers, implants, or Invisalign, treatment typically begins at a separate scheduled visit after the plan is finalized.

Candidacy and Safety

Not every patient is a candidate for every procedure. If a treatment is not right for you, we will tell you honestly and explain what alternatives may make sense. Patient safety and long-term oral health always come first.
