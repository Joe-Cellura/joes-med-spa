Team Overview

Every patient at Bright Smile Dental is cared for by licensed dental professionals. You will always know who is treating you and why.

Providers

Dr. James Park, DDS — Lead Dentist & Practice Owner
Dr. Park has over 12 years of experience in general and cosmetic dentistry. He founded Bright Smile Dental with a focus on patient comfort, honest recommendations, and natural-looking results. Dr. Park performs general, restorative, and implant dentistry. He is known for taking time to explain treatment options clearly and never rushing patients into decisions they are not ready for.

Dr. Maya Patel, DDS — Cosmetic Dentist
Dr. Patel specializes in veneers, whitening, and smile design. She has an eye for aesthetics and a gentle chairside manner. Dr. Patel works closely with patients to understand their vision for their smile and develops plans that achieve natural, beautiful outcomes without looking overdone.

Sarah Kim, RDH — Lead Dental Hygienist
Sarah has been with the practice since day one. She performs cleanings, periodontal care, and patient education. Sarah's approach to hygiene visits is thorough but relaxed — many anxious patients say she is the reason they stopped dreading the dentist.

Marcus Johnson — Patient Coordinator
Marcus handles scheduling, insurance verification, and the flow of every patient visit. He is the first face patients see and ensures each visit starts with a warm, organized welcome. Marcus is the person to call with questions about appointments, pricing, or insurance.

Team Philosophy

The Bright Smile team believes dental care should feel as good as it looks. That means honest communication, comfortable procedures, and treatment plans that are genuinely in the patient's best interest — not designed to maximize revenue. Patients are never pressured to begin treatment before they are ready, and every recommendation comes with a clear explanation.

Consultations and Care Coordination

New patients always meet with Dr. Park or Dr. Patel before beginning any treatment. During the consultation, the provider reviews oral health history, discusses goals, and creates a personalized plan. Marcus coordinates follow-up scheduling, insurance questions, and any logistics from that point forward.
