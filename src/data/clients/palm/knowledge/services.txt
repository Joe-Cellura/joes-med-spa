Palm Concierge Agency — Services

NANNY PLACEMENT
Registration fee: $250 (non-refundable)
Placement fee: 4 weeks of nanny's agreed salary (registration fee deducted at placement)

Process:
- Family submits application and pays registration fee
- Family consultation to understand needs
- Family writes job description, approved and posted by agency
- Agency conducts candidate interviews
- Family interviews top candidates in person or via Zoom
- Family selects preferred candidate
- Paid trial day arranged
- Work agreement signed by both parties
- Family sends written contract to nanny
- Final placement fee invoiced and paid
- Placement complete

BABYSITTING PLACEMENT
Registration fee: $100 (non-refundable)
Up to 2 sitters included in standard process.
Additional sitters: $50 per sitter. Maximum 4 sitters per family.

Process:
- Family submits application and pays registration fee
- Family consultation
- Family writes job description
- Agency interviews candidates
- Family interviews top candidates
- Paid trial arranged
- Work agreement signed by both parties
- Family moves forward with up to 2 sitters for on-call needs

DOG WALKER / DOG SITTER
Registration fee: $50 for dog walker, $75 for dog sitter (one-time, non-refundable)
Ongoing rates: Agreed directly between client and professional. Payments made directly to employee.

Features:
- Experienced, background-checked walkers and sitters
- Flexible scheduling (daily, weekly, or one-time)
- Tailored to dog's needs, breed, size, and temperament
- Safe and fun for all dogs

ERRAND RUNNER / HOME ORGANIZER
