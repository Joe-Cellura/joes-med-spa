Palm Concierge Agency — Pricing

REGISTRATION FEES (one-time, non-refundable)
Nanny placement: $250
Babysitting placement: $100
Dog walker: $50
Dog sitter: $75
Errand runner / home organizer: $100–$150 depending on needs

PLACEMENT FEES
Nanny: Final placement fee = 4 weeks of nanny's agreed salary. The $250 registration fee is deducted from this total.
Babysitting: No separate placement fee beyond registration. Additional sitters beyond 2: $50 per sitter. Maximum 4 sitters per family.

ONGOING RATES
Dog walking, dog sitting, errand running, and home organizing:
- Ongoing pay rates are agreed directly between the client and the professional
- Payments are made directly to the employee
- Palm Concierge Agency does not invoice for ongoing services

NOTE: Palm Concierge Agency does not publish hourly or session rates for ongoing services. These are negotiated between the client and professional after placement.
