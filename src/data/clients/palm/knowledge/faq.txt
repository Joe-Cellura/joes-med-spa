Palm Concierge Agency — FAQ

Q: What services do you offer?
A: Nanny placement, babysitting placement, dog walking and sitting, and errand running and home organization.

Q: How do I get started?
A: Submit a family application at https://form.jotform.com/242545954800156 or contact us at +1 631-901-3328 or palmconciergeagency@gmail.com.

Q: How do you ensure quality?
A: Every professional undergoes background checks, reference checks, and qualification verification.

Q: Can I meet the professional before committing?
A: Yes — we encourage families to meet candidates to ensure the right fit.

Q: What areas do you serve?
A: Tampa Bay Area, Florida only.

Q: Are there registration fees?
A: Yes — all non-refundable:
- Nanny: $250
- Babysitting: $100
- Dog walker: $50 / Dog sitter: $75
- Errand runner/organizer: $100–$150

Q: What is the nanny placement fee?
A: 4 weeks of the nanny's agreed salary. The $250 registration fee is deducted from this total.

Q: How are ongoing payments handled for dog walking and errand running?
A: Ongoing rates are agreed directly between client and professional. Payments go directly to the employee — Palm does not invoice for ongoing services.

Q: Is there a cap on babysitters per family?
A: Yes — 4 maximum. The first 2 are included in the standard process. Each additional sitter is $50.

Q: Do you serve Long Island or New York?
A: We currently only serve the Tampa Bay Area in Florida.
