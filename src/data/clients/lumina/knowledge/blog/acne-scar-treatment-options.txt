Acne Scar Treatment Options: What We Offer

Lumina Aesthetics — Raleigh, North Carolina

Introduction

Acne scars can affect texture and confidence. Lumina offers treatments that may improve the appearance of acne scarring—we do not diagnose or treat active acne as a disease; that is for a physician or dermatologist. Here’s how we can help within our scope.

Microneedling

Microneedling creates controlled micro-channels in the skin to support collagen and improve texture. It can help soften the look of shallow acne scars and uneven texture. A series of sessions is often recommended. We will assess your skin and recommend a plan.

Chemical Peels

Chemical peels can improve tone and texture and may help with the appearance of post-acne marks and shallow scarring. Depth and type of peel depend on your skin. We will recommend what is safe and effective for you.

Laser and Light-Based Treatments

Depending on your skin type and goals, we may offer laser or light-based options that can improve texture and the look of scarring. Candidacy depends on your skin; we will assess at your consultation.

Why a Personalized Plan Matters

Not every treatment is right for every skin type or scar type. We need to see your skin, understand your history, and recommend a plan that is safe and realistic. Some clients also benefit from working with a dermatologist for prescription or procedural options—we can advise when a referral may be appropriate.

What to Expect

Results are gradual. Multiple sessions are often needed. We will give you a realistic timeline and aftercare so you can protect your results. We do not guarantee specific improvement; we aim to support the best outcome within our scope.
