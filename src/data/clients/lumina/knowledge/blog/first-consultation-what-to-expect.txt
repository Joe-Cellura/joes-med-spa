Your First Consultation: What to Expect

Lumina Aesthetics — Raleigh, North Carolina

Introduction

Your first visit to Lumina is a consultation—a chance for us to learn your goals and for you to learn what we offer. You are not obligated to book treatment the same day. Here’s what typically happens.

Before You Arrive

You may receive intake forms to complete. These help us understand your medical history, medications, and skin concerns. If you have questions about what to list, we can clarify when you arrive.

When You Arrive

You will check in and may meet with a consultation coordinator who can answer general questions and make sure we have what we need. Then you will meet with a clinical provider—a physician, PA, or in some cases another qualified team member—who will discuss your goals and assess the areas you want to address.

The Conversation

We will ask what you want to improve or maintain, what your timeline is (e.g., an event), and how much downtime you can accommodate. We will review your history and any current products or treatments. We will look at your face or skin as needed to recommend options. You can ask anything—we are here to educate and recommend, not to pressure.

Recommendations and Pricing

We will recommend treatments that fit your goals, anatomy, and situation. We will explain what each option involves, typical downtime, and results. We will also give you pricing—transparent and upfront. If you want to stage treatment over several visits, we can outline that.

Same-Day Treatment

For some services, same-day treatment may be an option if you are a good candidate and have had time to consider the plan. It is never required. For filler or more involved treatments, we often suggest a separate visit so you are not rushed.

After the Consultation

You can book a treatment visit, schedule another consultation, or take time to decide. We are happy to answer follow-up questions by phone or email. There is no pressure to commit on the spot.

Summary

Your first visit is about learning and planning. We want you to leave informed and confident, whether you book that day or later.
