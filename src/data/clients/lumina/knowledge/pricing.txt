CLIENT PRICING OVERVIEW — LUMINA AESTHETICS

Consolidated from Lumina knowledge/services/*.txt, treatments.json, knowledge/faq.txt, knowledge/consultation-process.txt, and related bundle files. Ranges are approximate where stated in sources.

GENERAL PRICING NOTES
• knowledge/faq.txt & consultation-process.txt: Pricing is discussed during consultation; you receive an estimate before treatment. Injectables may be per unit or per area; skin and body treatments may be per session or as a series. Transparent; no hidden fees (faq.txt).
• Neurotoxin service file: Total cost depends on units used; recommended units and total cost are discussed before treatment.
• Hydrafacial service file: Quote at booking or check-in for that visit.
• Several services say “we will quote at your consultation” in their service descriptions.

INJECTABLES
• Service: Neuromodulators (neurotoxins)
Pricing: Typically per unit. knowledge/services/neurotoxins.txt: approximately $12–18 per unit depending on product and area. treatments.json lists Neuromodulators as “from $14/unit” (starting-at label; falls within the stated per-unit range in long-form copy).
Notes: Total cost depends on units used. Conservative dosing stated in neurotoxins.txt.

• Service: Dermal filler
Pricing: Typically per syringe or per area. knowledge/services/dermal-fillers.txt: often approximately $750–1,200+ per syringe depending on product and area. treatments.json lists dermal filler as “from $750.”
Notes: Multiple areas or syringes increase cost. Estimate at consultation (dermal-fillers.txt).
