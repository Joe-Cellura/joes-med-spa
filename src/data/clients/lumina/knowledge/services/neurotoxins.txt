Lumina Aesthetics — Neurotoxins (Neuromodulators)

Overview

Neuromodulators are injectable treatments that temporarily reduce muscle movement in the face. They are commonly used for forehead lines, frown lines between the brows, and crow's feet. At Lumina we use FDA-approved neurotoxins and focus on subtle, natural-looking movement—you can still express; lines are softened, not frozen.

Who It's For

Adults who want to soften dynamic lines (lines that appear when you move your face) without a frozen or overdone look. Good candidates are generally in good health and have realistic expectations. We do not treat pregnant or breastfeeding individuals or those with certain neurological conditions.

Common Goals

Soften forehead lines, frown lines, or crow's feet; prevent lines from deepening; achieve a refreshed look with minimal obvious change.

What Treatment Feels Like

Treatment involves small injections with a fine needle. Discomfort is usually mild and brief. Some providers use ice or topical numbing. The appointment is quick once the product is placed.

Typical Appointment Length

15–30 minutes including consultation and aftercare review. First-time clients may need a bit longer for discussion and marking.

Downtime

Minimal. You can usually return to normal activities the same day. We ask you to avoid rubbing the area, lying flat for a few hours, and strenuous exercise for 24 hours. Small bumps may be visible for a short time; they resolve quickly.

Results Timeline

Results typically begin within a few days, with full effect around 7–14 days. You will see gradual softening of movement and lines. If you need a touch-up, we can discuss it at a follow-up.

Pricing Range

Pricing is typically per unit. At Lumina, neurotoxin is often in the range of approximately $12–18 per unit, depending on product and area. Total cost depends on how many units are used, which we discuss at your consultation. We use conservative dosing; we do not over-treat to increase cost.

Consultation Guidance

We review your goals, movement patterns, and anatomy to plan placement and dose. We will tell you how many units we recommend and the total cost before treatment. Same-day treatment may be available for appropriate candidates.

Aftercare Notes

Avoid rubbing or massaging the treated areas for several hours. Avoid lying flat for a few hours. Skip strenuous exercise and excessive heat for 24 hours. We provide written aftercare and are available for questions.

When This May Be a Good Fit

When you want to soften lines with minimal downtime and a natural result. When you have time for a brief consultation and are comfortable with injectable treatment.

When a Consultation Matters Most

First-time neurotoxin, history of adverse reaction, or if you are unsure about areas or dosing. We will assess and recommend; we never pressure more units than you need.
