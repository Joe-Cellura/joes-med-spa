Lumina Aesthetics — Body Contouring

Overview

Non-surgical body contouring at Lumina refers to treatments that aim to reduce fat or improve the appearance of contours without surgery. We use devices that are cleared for this purpose and that fit our safety and outcome standards. Results are gradual and vary by person; we set realistic expectations during your consultation.

Who It's For

Adults who want to reduce stubborn fat or improve contour in treatable areas and who understand that results are not equivalent to surgery. Good candidates are generally near their goal weight and have realistic expectations. We assess health and anatomy to determine candidacy.

Common Goals

Reduce fat in areas such as abdomen, flanks, or thighs; smooth contours; complement diet and exercise when results have plateaued.

What Treatment Feels Like

Depends on the device. Some treatments involve cooling or heating; you may feel pulling, cold, or warmth. We explain what to expect and can often adjust for comfort. Treatment is non-invasive; no incisions.

Typical Appointment Length

30–60 minutes per area depending on the technology. Consultation is separate and may be 15–30 minutes.

Downtime

Usually minimal. Some devices have no downtime; others may cause temporary redness, tenderness, or mild swelling. We will explain what to expect for the specific treatment we recommend.

Results Timeline

Results are gradual as the body processes affected fat cells. Many clients start to see change in several weeks, with full effect over a few months. Multiple sessions may be recommended. We do not promise specific inch or weight loss; we describe typical outcomes.

Pricing Range

At Lumina, body contouring is typically priced per area or per session. Single sessions may start in the range of approximately $499–1,000+ depending on technology and area. Packages may be available. We will quote at your consultation.

Consultation Guidance

We review your goals, health, and the areas you want to treat. We explain which technology we use, how many sessions we typically recommend, and what results to expect. We will tell you if we do not think you are a good candidate.

Aftercare Notes

We provide device-specific aftercare. This may include staying hydrated, avoiding certain activities, or wearing compression if recommended. Follow-up sessions are scheduled as needed.

When This May Be a Good Fit

When you have stubborn areas that have not responded to diet and exercise and you want a non-surgical option. When you can commit to a series and have realistic expectations.

When a Consultation Matters Most

Always. Body contouring is not right for everyone. We need to assess your health, anatomy, and goals and recommend the right treatment and number of sessions.
