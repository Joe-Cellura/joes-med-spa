Frequently Asked Questions — Glo de Vie Med Spa

Q: Who performs treatments?
A: All treatments are performed by licensed medical professionals including Amanda Gallagher-Dunning MS APRN-BC and our medical aesthetics team, overseen by Medical Director Dr. Benjamin Eskra MD.

Q: Do you offer Botox?
A: Yes. We offer Botox and Dysport as neuromodulators for softening fine lines and wrinkles, as well as a full range of dermal fillers for volume and contouring.

Q: What is EMFACE?
A: EMFACE is a non-invasive facial treatment using RF energy and HIFES technology to simultaneously treat skin and facial muscles, reducing wrinkles and lifting contours without needles.

Q: Do you offer body contouring?
A: Yes. We offer Exilis Ultra for fat reduction and skin tightening, and Emsculpt for muscle toning and fat reduction. Both are non-surgical with no downtime.
