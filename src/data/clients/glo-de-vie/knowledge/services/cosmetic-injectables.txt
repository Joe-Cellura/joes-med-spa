Cosmetic Injectables at Glo de Vie Med Spa

Services: Botox, Dysport, and dermal fillers.

Neuromodulators (Botox and Dysport) temporarily relax the muscles that cause dynamic lines including forehead lines, frown lines between the brows, and crow's feet around the eyes. Results typically last 3 to 4 months.

Dermal fillers restore lost volume, smooth static lines, and enhance facial contours. Options include lip enhancement, cheek volume, under-eye hollows, and jawline definition.

All injectable treatments are customized to the client's facial structure and goals with a focus on natural, balanced results.
