EMFACE at Glo de Vie Med Spa

EMFACE is a non-invasive facial treatment that simultaneously delivers radiofrequency energy and HIFES (high-intensity facial electrical stimulation) technology. It treats both the skin surface and the underlying facial muscles in the same session.

Benefits include reduced wrinkles, improved skin elasticity, and lifted facial contours — without needles or downtime. Sessions are approximately 20 minutes. Most clients complete a series of 4 sessions.

EMFACE is an alternative to injectables for clients who want facial improvement without needles, or a complement to injectable treatments for more comprehensive results.
