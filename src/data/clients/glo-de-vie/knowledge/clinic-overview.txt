Glo de Vie Med Spa Overview

Glo de Vie Med Spa is a boutique medical spa located in the North Hills area of Raleigh, North Carolina. The practice offers a wide range of non-surgical aesthetic treatments in an intimate, relaxing environment.

Address: 4209 Lassiter Mill Rd, Suite 133, Raleigh, NC 27609
Phone: (919) 510-5919
Hours: Monday: 8am to 7pm. Tuesday: 8am to 7pm. Wednesday: 8am to 5pm. Thursday: 8am to 7pm. Friday: 8am to 5pm. Saturday: 9am to 3pm. Sunday: Closed.

Glo de Vie was founded with a mission to provide state-of-the-art corrective and therapeutic skin rejuvenation and contouring treatments. The practice is passionate about delivering safe, customized, results-driven treatments in a boutique setting that fosters long-lasting client relationships.

Services include cosmetic injectables, laser therapy, skin rejuvenation, EMFACE, body transformation with Exilis Ultra and Emsculpt, skin resurfacing, chemical peels, facials, advanced skin correction, and weight loss injections.

Pricing Policy

Glo de Vie Med Spa does not publish specific pricing 
online as treatment costs vary based on individual 
goals, areas treated, and personalized plans. Pricing 
is discussed transparently during a consultation. 
Most clients appreciate that this allows us to tailor 
everything specifically to their goals rather than 
using a one-size-fits-all price. Clients are encouraged 
to request a visit to receive a clear, personalized 
quote before committing to any treatment.
